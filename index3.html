When the days are tough and the moments feel long, Know you're not alone, you’ve always been strong. Though your body may ache and the road seem unclear, You’ve got the strength to face it, I’m always near. With every challenge, you stand tall and brave, Fighting through the storms, every wave you brave. The hard times will pass, the pain will subside, And you’ll rise again, with courage as your guide. There’s power in you, quiet and bright, More than you realize, you’re full of light. When it feels overwhelming, and you start to sway, I’m here for you, every step of the way. No moment too dark, no pain too deep, I’m with you always, even in your sleep. You’re stronger than you know, with every breath, And I’ll stand by you, through every step.
